ABOUT

Hi there, my name is Yuriy, and you are on my website, written by myself using JavaScript, CSS and, of course, HTML, first of all, the purpose of its creation was to apply theoretical knowledge of programming in practice, and secondly, it is the desire to have my own site with my own photos, I've had a penchant for photos since my school years, although I didn't have my own camera then, I borrowed it from my friend Dima, or went with him to take photos of local landscapes, although the first photos were ordinary plants, tree leaves, all kinds of insects and animals, probably every beginner photographer photographed it, I don't know.

Not much time passed when I decided to buy my own camera, it was and is a Nikon D3300, it was from it that the photos posted on this website were taken, the camera is good, but it has its disadvantages, for example - it is not convenient to take pictures from a low position, because a small clumsy screen, and the viewfinder also has a poor view, it is difficult to understand where the focus is, because of this I do not use lenses with manual focus, of course these disadvantages became clear after a long time of use, but now I know which next camera I will choose, I have several lenses, the first two with an aperture of 1.8 are 50mm and 35mm, and the usual 18-55.
